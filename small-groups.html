The idea

What a
group is.

We believe that the things you enjoy and care about are the very things that can make up your Small Group. You don't need a new life to join one — you bring the one you have.

Groups meet in homes, coffee shops, parks, and living rooms. They cook together, study Scripture together, carry each other's weight, and pray for each other by name. This is discipleship the way Jesus did it — in the ordinary rhythms of life.
