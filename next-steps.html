Four steps
one journey.

Growth Track takes you from first steps with Jesus to a life of purpose and ministry — one step at a time, at your own pace.

THE track.

01
STEP 1

KNOW GOD

The first step is knowing God personally — not just knowing about Him. Our services and Discover Newlife sessions will introduce you to the heart and mission of Newlife United.

STEP 2

FIND FREEDOM

Join a Small Group! We want people to gather together based on things they enjoy and are important to them. Essentially, the things you are already doing in your life are the things that can make up your Small Group. Find Freedom as you walk together, support and disciple each other on your journey with God.

02
03
STEP 3

DISCOVER PURPOSE

We believe every person has dreams and gifts that God has placed within them and want to see those gifts developed by being used to further God's kingdom. It's only when you discover your God-given purpose that you find joy in being who God has created you to be. Growth Track will enable you to find your God-given purpose.

STEP 4

MAKE A DIFFERENCE

Join the Dream Team! All of us were created by God to make a difference in the world around us. God has a place for you where your unique abilities and passions can impact the lives of others. In the Dream Team you will begin to serve others with your gifts, bringing your life purpose.
