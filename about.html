§ 01 — Our story

From a living room
to a house of nations.

Newlife United started in a living room as a small group of people hungry for more of God. What began with simple faith and a shared passion for prayer grew into a movement.

We are an ordinary church believing for a supernatural God to do the impossible — in homes, in workplaces, in prisons, across borders. What He did in scripture, He is still doing now.

"My house shall be called a house of prayer for all nations."
— MARK 11:17

§ 03 — What we believe

Plain truth, plainly held.

THE WORD

The Bible is the infallible, inspired Word of God — undeniable, irrevocable, alive. Our final authority in all matters of faith and practice.

2 TIM 3:16
THE GODHEAD

One God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit. Equal in power, glory and essence.

MATT 28:19
SALVATION

Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone — not by works, but as a gift to be received.

EPH 2:8-9
THE HOLY SPIRIT

We believe in the baptism and gifts of the Holy Spirit, given to empower the church for witness and service today.

ACTS 1:8
THE CHURCH

The church is the body of Christ — called to worship, disciple, send, and prepare a generation for His return.

EPH 4:11-13
HIS RETURN

Jesus is coming again — bodily, visibly, victoriously. We live ready, work urgently, and look up.

ACTS 1:11
