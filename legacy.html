ROMANS 12:6–8

"Having gifts that differ according to the grace given to us, let us use them: if giving, in generosity… if leading, with zeal… if showing mercy, with cheerfulness."

The gift of giving — financial, experiential, relational — is one of the most powerful gifts in the body. The Legacy Team is a community of people who have lived long enough to know what that means, and are committed to using it.

This is not a retirement from ministry. It is the fullest expression of it.
