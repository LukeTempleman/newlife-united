Age groups

BABIES & TODDLERS · 0 – 3
Nursery

JUNIORS · GR R – GR 3
Juniors

Songs, stories, crafts, and the kind of fun that makes kids ask to come back. Age-appropriate Bible teaching delivered in ways that stick — visual, active, and real.

SENIORS · GR 4 – GR 7

Bigger conversations for bigger kids. Worship, teaching, and small-group discussion that takes the questions of pre-teens seriously and points them to Truth.

Safety first

Safe and
known.

Your child's safety is not an afterthought — it is the first thing we get right.

01
Background-checked volunteers
Every adult working with children in Kids For Christ has completed a police clearance check and our internal Safe Church training.
02
Check-in system
Each child gets a unique label on drop-off. You keep the matching stub — no stub, no collection. It's simple and it works.
03
Two adults per room, always
No child is ever alone with a single adult. Two volunteers are present at all times in every room.
04
No pressure drop-off
Babies and toddlers are completely welcome in the main service. You decide what works for your family — we follow your lead.
