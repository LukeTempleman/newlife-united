WHAT WE'RE
building.

IDENTITY
Who you are

The lie the world tells teenagers is that identity is something you invent. We teach that identity is something you discover — in God, who made you on purpose and calls you by name.

COMMUNITY
Who you're with

Teenagers grow in groups. Youth United builds friendships that go beyond Friday night — a crew that shows up, tells the truth, and runs the same race together.

PURPOSE
What you're for

We raise disciples, not spectators. Youth United teenagers lead worship, serve in Kids For Christ, and go on missions — because the calling to make a difference doesn't start at 18.

For parents

A note
from us.

We know what it costs to hand your teenager over to a community that isn't you. We take that seriously.

Every youth leader is background-checked, trained, and accountable to pastoral oversight. We don't just love your kids on Friday night — we pray for them by name through the week.

We also believe that parents are the primary disciplers. We're here to come alongside you, not replace you. If you want to connect with the youth team, meet the leaders, or talk about your teenager — contact us. We mean that.
