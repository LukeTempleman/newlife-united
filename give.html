A response, not a requirement

Why we give.

Generosity is one of the clearest pictures of who God is. He gave first. Everything we have is His to begin with — and when we give back, we're agreeing with that. There's no pressure to give, and visitors should feel absolutely none. If you call Newlife United home, this is how we fund the family.

"Each one must give as he has decided in his heart, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."
2 CORINTHIANS 9:7
